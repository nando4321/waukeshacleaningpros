What drives the cost of commercial cleaning?

1. Square footage

More space takes more time, but large open areas often clean faster per square foot than the same area split into small offices and conference rooms.

2. Facility type

Medical, industrial, restaurant, school, retail, and general office spaces each have different cleaning requirements and price ranges.

3. Cleaning frequency

More visits per week usually lowers the cost per visit because the building stays cleaner between services.

4. Day vs. night cleaning

After-hours cleaning is usually more efficient because crews can access the space without working around employees or customers.

5. Scope of services

Recurring cleaning, floor care, carpet extraction, post-construction cleanup, window washing, and disinfection all price differently.

6. Waukesha-area factors

Winter salt, local travel distance, building traffic, and labor quality all affect cleaning plans in southeastern Wisconsin.
