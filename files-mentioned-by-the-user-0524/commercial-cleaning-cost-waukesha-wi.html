Price Factors

What affects your final quote?

Square footage

Large open spaces often clean faster than the same square footage split into many small rooms.

Facility type

Medical, industrial, restaurant, school, retail, and office spaces each require different standards.

Cleaning frequency

More visits per week usually lowers the cost per visit because the building stays cleaner between services.

Scope of work

Restrooms, breakrooms, hard floors, carpet, glass, disinfection, and specialty tasks all affect the quote.

Before You Hire

Questions to ask any cleaning company

  • Are you bonded and insured?
  • Do you run background checks on staff?
  • Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
  • Who do I call if something goes wrong?
  • What does your satisfaction guarantee cover?
Important

The cheapest bid is not always the lowest cost.

Very low bids often mean rushed work, missed details, frequent staff turnover, or inconsistent quality. A dependable cleaning plan should include clear scope, local communication, quality checks, and transparent pricing.

FAQs

Commercial cleaning pricing questions

How much does commercial cleaning cost per square foot in Waukesha?

Most recurring commercial cleaning runs $0.07-$0.18 per square foot per visit, depending on facility type, frequency, and scope.

How often should my office be cleaned?

Many professional offices do well with 3x/week service. Medical, school, food-service, and high-traffic facilities often need daily cleaning.

Do you require a long-term contract?

We can discuss month-to-month and longer-term arrangements. The right fit depends on the facility, scope, and cleaning frequency.

What is included in standard commercial cleaning?

Typical recurring service includes floor care, trash removal, restroom cleaning, surface dusting, breakroom wipe-down, and high-touch disinfection.
